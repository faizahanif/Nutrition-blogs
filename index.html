    üç≥ Healthy Breakfast Recipe

    Vegetable Omelet with Whole Wheat Toast

    Modal title

    Vegetable Omelet with Whole Wheat Toast

    Recipe

    Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 slice whole wheat bread
    •

    Method:

    1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk well.
    2. Add chopped onion, tomato, salt, and black pepper.
    3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook until set.
    5. Flip and cook the other side.
    6. Lightly toast the whole wheat bread and serve together.

    Health Benefits:

  • High in protein
  • Keeps you full for longer
  • Boosts morning energy
    •
